An iPhone is a smartphone made by Apple that combines a computer, digital camera, iPod, and cellular phone into one device. It has a touchscreen interface and runs on Apple's iOS operating system. The iPhone is a line of smartphones designed and marketed by Apple. The first iPhone was released in 2007, and there have been many subsequent models released since then. The iPhone is one of the most popular smartphones in the world, and it has had a significant impact on the mobile phone industry.

Key moment of iphone history


  • 2007: The first iPhone is released. It is a revolutionary device that combines a mobile phone, a music player, and an internet communicator into one device.

  • 2008: The App Store is launched. This allows developers to create and distribute apps for the iPhone, which greatly expands the functionality of the device.

  • 2010: The iPhone 4 is released. It features a new design and a higher-resolution screen.

  • 2011: The iPhone 4S is released. It introduces Siri, a voice-activated personal assistant.

  • 2012: The iPhone 5 is released. It features a larger screen and a faster processor.

  • 2014: The iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus are released. They feature even larger screens and a new design.

  • 2015: The iPhone 6S and iPhone 6S Plus are released. They introduce 3D Touch, a new technology that allows the screen to sense pressure.

  • 2016: The iPhone SE is released. It is a smaller and less expensive version of the iPhone 6S.

  • 2017: The iPhone X is released. It features a new design with an edge-to-edge display and Face ID, a facial recognition system.

  • 2017: The iPhone X is released. It features a new design with an edge-to-edge display and Face ID, a facial recognition system.

  • 2018: The iPhone XS and iPhone XS Max are released. They feature even larger screens and improved cameras.

  • 2019: The iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro, and iPhone 11 Pro Max are released. They feature improved cameras and processors.

  • 2020: The iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Mini, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPhone 12 Pro Max are released. They feature a new design with flat edges and 5G connectivity.

  • 2022: The iPhone 14, iPhone 14 Plus, iPhone 14 Pro, and iPhone 14 Pro Max are released. They feature a new design with a notchless display and an always-on display.

  • 2023: The iPhone 15, iPhone 15 Plus, iPhone 15 Pro, and iPhone 15 Pro Max are released. They feature a new design with a titanium frame and a periscope camera.
